This demo is based on the GD32VF103V-EVAL-V1.0 board, it shows how to use the USART DMA transmit and receive.

Firstly, the USART sends the strings to the hyperterminal and then loops waiting for receiving max 10 bytes data from the hyperterminal.If the number of data you enter equal with or more than 10 bytes, USART will send 10 bytes to the hyperterminal.

JP5 and JP6 must be fitted.
