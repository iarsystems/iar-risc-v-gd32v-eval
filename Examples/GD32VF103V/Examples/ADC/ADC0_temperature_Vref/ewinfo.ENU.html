This demo is based on the GD32VF103V-EVAL-1.0 board, it shows how to use the ADC to convert analog signal to digital data. The ADC is configured in dependent mode, inner channel16(temperature sensor channel) and channel17(Vref channel) are chosen as analog input pin.

As the ADC convertion begins by software, the converted data in the ADC_IDATAx register, where the x is 0 to 1.

The temperature can be watched and vref_value in debug mode or by COM. JP5 and JP6 jump to USART.
